With a campus near almost every Pennsylvania resident and global online options, Penn State's world-class education is available to meet students where they are. There is only one Penn State and the path toward a degree can start wherever is right for you.

One university. Many options.

Penn State's 2+2 Plan gives you the flexibility to start your studies at one campus and then transition to another campus that offers your major to complete your degree. This option ensures you can begin where it’s most convenient for you and finish at a campus that fits your academic goals.
